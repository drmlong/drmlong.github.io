I'm a Marie Skłodowska-Curie postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Edinburgh (funded by the UKRI Horizon Guarantee). You can read about my project 'GRASP: Greater Awareness of Social Pragmatics in Ageing' here: .

Prior to this fellowship I was a visiting postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics investigating multimodal communication through video and portable eye-tracking methods, and a postdoc at the University of Oslo examining referential communication across distinct populations, languages, and ages. My PhD thesis (at Edinburgh Uni) examined the cognitive mechanisms underlying perspective-taking over the lifespan and the impact of later-life language learning on cognitive ageing.
